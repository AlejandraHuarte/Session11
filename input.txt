Bob bid big, but bot bad bag!
Bob bit bay, bar; but bot bat.
Bin bit bog, bit; but bog bid.
Bus bit big, bay: but bob bot?
Bat bit bid, bob; but bog bay.
Bin bid bar, but bat bot bag!
Bay big bin, bot: but bid bog.
Bot bat big, but bag bit bus.
Bag bin bot, but bar bid bat.
Big bus bit bot, bay bid bin.
Bar bag bit bot; but bin bat?
Bit bat bin bot: but bar bay!
Bay bat bid bin, but bog bus.
Bob bit bat bar, but bin bot.
Bat bag bit bin! But bay bot?
Bin bat bid bus, but bar bag.
Bit bat bin bag, but bay bid!
Bus bay bit bat; but bar bin.
Bob bid bag, but bit big bat!
Bar bay bin bot, but bus bit?
